Stanislav Wasserbauer

Maker & 3D specialist at Prusa Labs

Stanislav is a product designer focusing on rapid prototyping, technical 3D modelling & 3D print, and laser CNC. He is familiar with working with various materials - wood, concrete, iron.
He currently works at PrusaLab where he works for clients such as Skoda Auto, but also for startups through their accelerator programs. He is able to help clients with technical 3D modelling, 3D print and rapid prototyping. In other words, he is able to create a prototype for almost any product that has a physical form.
